# Third-Party Packs

JoyBoy supports optional local packs. Packs can add routing rules, prompt assets, model sources, UI overrides, or experimental workflows without modifying the public core.

Third-party packs are not part of JoyBoy's public core. Review any pack before installing it, and only use packs that comply with your local laws, model licenses, and hosting platform policies.

## Installing a pack

1. Download the pack zip or folder.
2. Open JoyBoy.
3. Open `Addons` from the sidebar.
4. Open the local packs section.
5. Import the zip or local folder.
6. Activate the pack.

JoyBoy expects a pack to contain a `pack.json` manifest and an `assets/` folder.

## Mature / Adult local packs

Some optional local packs may target mature or adult workflows where legal, consensual, and compliant with platform policies. These packs are not part of the public core.

Rules of thumb:

- do not use packs for non-consensual intimate imagery;
- do not use packs for minors or ambiguous-age subjects;
- do not publish explicit preview media in the public JoyBoy README;
- keep mature packs opt-in and separate from the public core;
- follow the model license and the hosting platform rules.
